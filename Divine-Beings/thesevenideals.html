The Seven Ideals

Humankind had thought that there were divinities above them, but so too divines that empower and enslave them. To themselves, they are all of the pieces that give nature to humankind—but not just; even more, a refinement. An engreatening. A perfection to the ordinances of Providence. They are born from the Vessel, and reminisce of His interpersonal throes from the World. Likewise, within every man houses the seven’s conflict of both coexistence and difference. In virtue they bless, but in vice they take.
