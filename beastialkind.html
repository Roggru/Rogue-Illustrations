Of the

Beastialkind

Blessed be for those whose power shakes the very earth. The beasts of the World exist within its every corner, and every stretch and expanse imaginable as if caretakers to the divine beings and their makings. Yet, they are not gods, though have the might of one; not daemons, though contain a strength and terror far greater than them. They are nature's denizens who take after Her ruthlessness and reverence, and are a constant reminder that the World never belonged to humankind.

"Aieǐn sædā haenir."

Even stars burn.
